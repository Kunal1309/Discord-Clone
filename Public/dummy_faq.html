    Ques 2. What are the activities undertaken by the placement cell of UAS Bangalore?

    Ques 3. What activities does the placements cell conduct to prepare ICFAI University Raipur students for placement?

    Ans: the training and placement cell of ICFAI has senior professionals and placement executives to train students. The team evaluates performance of the students and ensures students corporate placements. The team plays a vital role in training students by working on both supply and demand sides of the placement.
